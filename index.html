xz/fonts

Break your users from unethical tracking networks. xz/fonts delivers exclusively open-source fonts through Vercel's impossibly fast CDN.

Want to see for yourself? Watch how fast fonts render on this page.

For the font Inter, it's as easy as adding this to your CSS:

@import url('https://fonts.xz.style/serve/inter.css');

or this to your HTML's <head>:

<link rel="stylesheet" href="https://fonts.xz.style/serve/inter.css">

Usage

Visit the documentation at docs.xz.style/fonts for an easy step-by-step guide.
